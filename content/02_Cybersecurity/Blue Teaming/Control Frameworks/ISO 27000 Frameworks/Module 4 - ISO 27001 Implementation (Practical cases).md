Statement of Applicability (Security controls list Annex A) - Is onboarded during Risk Analysis.
Before Auditing, the Auditor will ask for the SoA and those are the ones that are audited.
- **Annex A Controls:** Lists the 93 controls from ISO 27001:2022.
- **Applicability:** States whether each control applies to your organization.
- **Justification:** Provides reasoning for why a control is included (or excluded).
- **Implementation Status:** Documents whether the control is currently in place.

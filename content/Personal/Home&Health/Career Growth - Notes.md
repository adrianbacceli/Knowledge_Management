# 💼 Career Strategy & Notes

- ✅ **FAANG Remote Jobs**: Target senior positions in companies like Amazon, Google, etc.  
- 🔍 **Explore** [TechPays](https://techpays.com/) — Big salaries are possible, especially in cloud and security roles.  
- 🎯 **Growth Approach**: Keep learning and specializing, but avoid being boxed into a single niche.  
- 🧠 **Learn CyberChef**: Great tool for encoding/decoding and data analysis.  
- 📚 **Familiarize with** [LeetCode](https://leetcode.com/) for technical interviews.  
- 🧰 **Use Dradis** for professional pentest reporting.

# 🧠 Weekly Meal Execution Plan (Fat Loss / High Protein)

> [!tip] Helpful Tip  
> 💡 Stick to it for a month. Then adjust to variety, budget, diet needs

---
## 🛒 SUNDAY – SHOP + PREP DAY

### 🛒 Shopping List

#### Proteins
- [ ] 2 lbs chicken breast (boneless, skinless)
- [ ] 2 lbs lean ground beef
- [ ] 10–12 eggs
- [ ] 3–4 cans tuna
- [ ] 1 pack chicken or turkey sausage
- [ ] Protein powder (~7–10 scoops)

#### Carbs
- [ ] 2 cups oats
- [ ] 2 cups dry rice
- [ ] 4–5 medium potatoes
- [ ] 4–6 bananas
- [ ] 2–3 apples
- [ ] 2–3 oranges
- [ ] 1 bag frozen berries (400–600g)
- [ ] 5–6 tortillas
- [ ] 1 box crackers

#### Extras
- [ ] Cheese (~200g, mozzarella or Swiss)
- [ ] Olive oil or PAM spray
- [ ] 2–3 onions
- [ ] 2–3 bell peppers
- [ ] Lettuce (optional)
- [ ] Peanut butter (optional)
- [ ] Seasonings (garlic powder, salt, pepper, paprika, cumin)

---

### 🍳 Meal Prep Instructions (After Shopping)

1. **Rice**
   - Cook 2 cups dry rice → yields ~5 cups
   - Cool, portion into 5 containers (1 cup each), refrigerate

2. **Chicken Breast**
   - Season with garlic, salt, pepper, paprika
   - Bake at 400°F / 200°C for ~25 min
   - Slice and portion into 4–5 meals (4–6 oz each)
   - Freeze 2 portions, refrigerate the rest

3. **Ground Beef**
   - Brown 2 lbs with chopped onion + dry seasoning
   - Portion into 3–4 containers (5–6 oz each)

4. **Potatoes**
   - Dice and roast (or boil) 4–5 potatoes
   - Add salt + olive oil/PAM
   - Portion into 3–4 containers

5. **Eggs**
   - Boil 10–12 eggs
   - Store in fridge (5-day max)

6. **Vegetables**
   - Dice 2 onions + 2 bell peppers
   - Store raw in airtight container
   - Wash and dry lettuce (optional), store with paper towel

7. **Oats**
   - Prepare 5 jars/containers:
     - ½ cup oats
     - 1 scoop protein powder
     - Add raisins OR prep fruit fresh
   - Store dry. Add liquid and fruit fresh daily.

8. **Cheese**
   - Grate or slice ~200g cheese
   - Store for wraps or eggs

9. **Freeze**
   - Slice and freeze ripe bananas
   - Freeze 2 portions of chicken breast
   - Freeze any leftover cooked rice if not needed soon

## 📅 DAILY ROUTINE

---

### 🗓️ MONDAY

#### 🌅 Morning
- 🥣 Grab oat jar → add 1 chopped banana + ½–1 cup milk → microwave 2 min
- 🍱 Grab beef + rice container, apple, 2 boiled eggs
- 👜 Pack for work

#### 🌙 Evening
- 🐔 Take 1 chicken breast portion from fridge
- 🥔 Take potato container out
- 🍳 Reheat both together for dinner
- 🍌 Set aside banana or apple for Tuesday snack
- 🌯 Pull 1 tortilla + cheese for Tuesday breakfast
- 🥄 Mix oats + protein in empty jar for Tuesday (if not prepped)

---

### 🗓️ TUESDAY

#### 🌅 Morning
- 🍳 Scramble 2 eggs + cheese, microwave or pan (5 min)
- 🌯 Heat tortilla, make breakfast wrap
- 🥫 Grab tuna can, 1 small tomato (slice at work), 1 cracker pack
- 🧃 Grab 1 orange
- 🍼 Mix 1 scoop protein + water in shaker (if drinking early)

#### 🌙 Evening
- 🥩 Pull ground beef portion for burger bowl
- 🧅 Chop lettuce/onion if not done
- 🥣 Set oat jar with raisins + protein for Wednesday
- 🐓 Move chicken breast from freezer to fridge for Thursday

---

### 🗓️ WEDNESDAY

#### 🌅 Morning
- 🥣 Add frozen berries + milk to oat jar → microwave
- 🍱 Grab chicken + rice container, 2 boiled eggs, banana

#### 🌙 Evening
- 🥩 Slice steak strips (if fresh) or defrost portion
- 🧅 Sauté steak with onion + bell peppers for dinner (10–12 min)
- 🧊 Freeze any leftover cooked meat you won’t eat this week
- 🍞 Set toast + eggs aside for Thursday morning

---

### 🗓️ THURSDAY

#### 🌅 Morning
- 🍳 Scramble 2 eggs, toast bread, slice banana
- 🥔 Reheat ground beef + potatoes for lunch
- 🍏 Grab apple, prep shake powder into shaker

#### 🌙 Evening
- 🌯 Prep tuna wrap (can mix tuna + cheese night before)
- 🧅 Add chopped tomato/onion
- 🥣 Mix oats + protein for Friday jar

---

### 🗓️ FRIDAY

#### 🌅 Morning
- 🥣 Add milk to oats + raisins, microwave
- 🍔 Cook burger patty (or reheat), pack with rice
- 🍱 Grab orange + 2 boiled eggs

#### 🌙 Evening
- 🐔 Reheat chicken + peppers for dinner
- 🍌 Grab banana, set oats + berries for Saturday
- 🍳 Pull eggs + tortilla for Saturday wrap

---

### 🗓️ SATURDAY

#### 🌅 Morning
- 🍳 Scramble eggs + cheese, wrap in tortilla
- 🥩 Reheat steak strips + rice for lunch
- 🍱 Grab banana + shake

#### 🌙 Evening
- 🌭 Cook chicken sausage, toss simple salad (lettuce + tomato)
- 🍎 Slice apple, pair with cheese
- 🍲 Set oat jar with protein and berries for Sunday

---

### 🗓️ SUNDAY

#### 🌅 Morning
- 🥣 Add milk to oat jar + microwave
- 🥫 Mix tuna + tomato/onion, reheat rice
- 🐔 Reheat chicken + potato for dinner

#### 🌙 Night
- ✅ Repeat **Prep + Shop** routine at top of file
- 🧼 Clean containers, reset fridge space

## 🧊 **Meal Prep Container List**

|📦 Item|Quantity|Purpose|Notes|
|---|---|---|---|
|**Large rectangular containers (3-compartment)**|3–4|Full meals (rice + meat + veg)|Great for lunches and dinners. BPA-free, microwave-safe recommended.|
|**Medium single-compartment containers (glass or plastic)**|4–6|Bulk meat, rice, or potatoes|Use for storing precooked chicken, beef, or rice.|
|**Small round or square containers (snack size, ~200–300ml)**|4–6|Snacks like boiled eggs, fruit, PB, raisins|Great for grab-and-go snacks. Stackable ones save space.|

|**Shaker bottle**|1–2|Protein shakes|Leak-proof, with mixer ball. Optional: keep one at work.|
|**Freezer-safe zip bags**|1 box|Frozen bananas, pre-cooked chicken/rice|Label and date. Flatten to stack.|
|**Cutlery set (portable)**|1|For work lunches|Or disposable sets if preferred.|
|**Mini sauce containers (optional)**|2–3|For peanut butter or dressing|Optional but handy for measured extras.|

---

## 💡 Tips for Choosing Containers

- **Microwave-safe**: Especially for lunch containers.
- **Glass vs. Plastic**:
    - Glass = better for hot foods, lasts longer
    - Plastic = lighter and cheaper for travel
- **Stackable design**: Saves fridge space
- **Dishwasher-safe**: Saves you time long-term

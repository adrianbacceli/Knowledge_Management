# Freelance Cybersecurity Consulting Using Free Tools (Panama)

## Objective
Start offering **cybersecurity consulting** in Panama using **free tools** (CIS CSAT Hosted + OpenVAS) to build a portfolio before transitioning to paid services.  
Initially focus only on **assessments and reports** (no implementation).

---

## 1. Core Service Offering (Free Tools)

| Tool | Purpose | Deliverable | Client Value |
|------|---------|-------------|--------------|
| **CIS CSAT Hosted** | Cybersecurity maturity self-assessment against CIS Controls (IG1–IG3) | PDF/Word report showing scores, gaps, recommendations | Management-friendly security posture overview |
| **OpenVAS** | Vulnerability scanning (internal/external) | Vulnerability report with risk ratings & remediation suggestions | Shows exploitable weaknesses |

---

## 2. Free-to-Paid Roadmap

### **Phase 1 – Free (Portfolio Building)**
- Target SMBs & NGOs for no-cost assessments.
- Deliver **professional reports** with:
  - Executive summary (non-technical, Spanish)
  - Findings & risk ratings
  - Remediation priority list
  - Methodology section referencing CIS & OpenVAS
- Collect testimonials & anonymized case studies.

### **Phase 2 – Low-Cost Entry**
- Charge $250–$500 for SMB assessments; $800+ for larger orgs.
- Offer quarterly reassessments.

### **Phase 3 – Premium Service**
- Add paid/licensed tools (CIS-CAT Pro, Nessus Pro, Qualys).
- Offer compliance mapping (PCI-DSS, ISO 27001, SBP).
- Package as annual retainers.

---

## 3. Engagement Workflow

1. **Intake & Scoping** → Asset count, business type, scope definition.
2. **CIS CSAT Assessment** → Complete IG1; record notes, evidence.
3. **OpenVAS Scan** → External & (if possible) authenticated scans.
4. **Reporting** → Merge both assessments into one branded PDF.
5. **Presentation** → Walkthrough + offer follow-up assessment.

---

## 4. Maximizing Impact with Free Tools
- Brand reports with logo/colors.
- Use templates for speed.
- Map results to known frameworks.
- Keep scope to SMBs for efficiency.
- Translate findings to **[[Business Impact Analysis|business impact]]**.

---

| Package Name | Tools | Price (after free phase) | Ideal Client |
|--------------|-------|--------------------------|--------------|
| Basic Security Health Check | CIS CSAT Hosted | $250–$400 | SMBs needing baseline awareness |

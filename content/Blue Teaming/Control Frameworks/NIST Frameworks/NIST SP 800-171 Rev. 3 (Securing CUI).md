NIST SP 800-171 Rev. 3 (Securing CUI / Non-fed)

Thursday, December 5, 2024

12:07 PM

Outlines specific steps for securing Controlled Unclassified Information (CUI) (Non-Federal systems). Here’s a simplified overview of the key steps:

1. Assess the Environment:

Identify where CUI resides, flows, and is used within your organization.

1. Identify Requirements:

Understand the specific security requirements detailed in NIST SP 800-171 Rev. 3, which are categorized into 14 families such as access control, incident response, and more.

1. Develop a System Security Plan (SSP):

Document how each security requirement will be implemented within your organization.

1. Implement Controls:

Apply the necessary security controls to protect CUI. This may involve technical solutions, policy changes, and staff training.

1. Conduct a Risk Assessment:

Evaluate risks to CUI and adjust controls as needed to mitigate these risks.

1. Monitor and Maintain:

Continuously monitor your security posture to ensure controls are effective and make improvements as needed.

1. Document Procedures:

Maintain documentation of policies, procedures, and compliance efforts to demonstrate that you meet the requirements.

1. Conduct Regular Reviews:

Periodically review and update your security practices and documentation to maintain ongoing compliance.

### 🔐 reCAPTCHA – Security Tool

> [!summary] Core Concept  
> reCAPTCHA is a risk analysis engine designed to protect websites from spam and malicious behavior by distinguishing between human and bot interactions.

### 🧠 How It Works

reCAPTCHA continuously analyzes patterns in user interactions to assess risk. It's commonly used to:

- Detect and block automated bots.
- Prevent spam in forms, reviews, and comments.
- Safeguard user authentication processes.
- Reduce fake friend requests or suspicious traffic.

---

### 🛒 **Example Use Case**

> Imagine managing an online store with product reviews.  
> reCAPTCHA automatically scans user-submitted reviews to flag suspicious content or bot activity, helping maintain the integrity of your site.
